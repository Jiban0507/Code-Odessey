◆ Our Story

Rooted in culture,
refined by passion

La Mera was born from a simple conviction: that Mexican cuisine deserves the same reverence as any great culinary tradition. Chef Valentina Cruz spent years tracing forgotten recipes across Oaxaca, Yucatán, and Veracruz to bring them to your table.

Every dish is a conversation between past and present — ancient chiles alongside modern technique, grandmother's mole with a chef's precision.
